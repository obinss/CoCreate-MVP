 The Pricing Calculation
How do we calculate the price when the material cost is effectively zero (or very low)? We don't price based on cost; we price based on value.
The Formula:
Final Price = (Logistics + Processing + Hardware) X 2.5
Example Calculation: The Garden Office Kit
	1	Logistics Cost: Van fuel + Driver time to pick up insulation/timber from big site.
	◦	Estimate: €40 per kit share.
	2	Processing Cost: Labor to sort, check, and bundle the kit.
	◦	Estimate: €30 (1 hour labor).
	3	Hardware Pack (New): Buying the screws and tape.
	◦	Estimate: €20.
	4	Total Base Cost: €90.
	5	Margin Multiplier (x 2.5) - is this sufficient to cover time and labor?:
	◦	Final Sale Price: €225.
Competitor Analysis (maybe we buy from restado.de)
	•	Buying these materials new at a hardware store would cost approx €550 - €600.
	•	Your Price (€225) is less than 50% of retail.
	•	Result: Massive savings for the customer, healthy margin for you.

How to Convince People (The Sales Strategy)
To sell "waste," you must tackle the psychological barriers.
1. The "Commercial Grade" Flex
	•	The Hook: "You can't buy this quality at [Local Hardware Store]."
	•	The Pitch: Explain that commercial sites use higher-spec materials than residential stores. The insulation is thicker; the steel is galvanized better; the wood is stress-graded. You are selling Upgrade material, not downgrade.
2. The "Hardware Pack" Trust Signal
	•	The Psychology: If you just sell a pile of wood, it looks like trash. If you include a branded box of brand new screws, brackets, and instructions, it becomes a Product.
	•	Strategy: Ensure the "Hardware Pack" is the first thing they see when they open the bundle. It signals: "We have thought this through."
3. The "Carbon Receipt"
	•	The Hook: "Build your office, save a forest."
	•	The Pitch: With every invoice, include a "Carbon Certificate" showing exactly how many kg of CO2 they saved by not buying new materials. People love sharing this on Instagram.
4. The "Limited Drop" Model (Scarcity)
	•	Don't have a permanent stock. Run "Drops."
	•	Email Blast: "Thursday Drop: We have 10 Granite BBQ Kits available. sourced from the new Hilton Hotel project. First come, first served."
	•	This turns "unpredictable inventory" into "exclusive access."
